About me

Hey there! I'm Albert, an independent digital creator and online entrepeneur based in Los Angeles, CA.
In addition to being an Environmental Studies graduate from UCSB, I have an extensive background in IT
as well E-Commerce, during which I developed the passion and skills for creating captivating content.

When I'm not putting my skills in data analytics or online business to use, I enjoy a wide variety of
hobbies and activites including, but not limited to, working out, photography, hiking, surfing, and going to
electronic music events with my friends. On more relaxing days, I am equally content spending time at
home watching movies, reading, or practicing yoga and meditation with my family.

As someone who loves exploring other cultures and traveling, I have the ambitious goal of
traveling to every country in the world and documenting my findings for people to see.
However, in an era where global conflict and distrust between different peoples and nations
seems to be growing exponentially by the day, my goal is not to simply become another one
of the millions of travel vloggers on the internet, but to foster constructive dialogue
on geopolitical issues so that we can create a more cooperative international community
where mutual respect and common prosperity are prioritized over expoitation and war.
